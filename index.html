My vinted account

Hello, I am Lilou. I created an account on Vinted last year.

1.The concept

It is really simple. If don't like your clothes, you can sell them on Vinted. Then, if someone like your clothes, you just have to send them to the person. It is cheap and sustainable !

2. How is it working ?

You choose the price of your clothes and take them in picture. Then, enveryone can see the clothes and can buy them. They pay you the price and shipping fees. As a seller, I have to wrap the clothes in a solid box and send them.

3. What I like about it

I really like this app. Thanks to Vinted, I make money and I make people happy. Sometimes, I even enjoy doing my shopping on Vinted too.
